About SSU

The Student Support Unit (SSU) is dedicated to supporting students throughout their academic journey. We provide guidance, advice, and assistance to help students overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.

Our Services

Academic Support

Guidance on coursework, study strategies, and improving academic performance.

Attendance Support

Help with attendance issues and understanding university requirements.

Personal Support

Confidential support for personal or family-related concerns.

Mental Health & Wellbeing

Support for stress, anxiety, and overall wellbeing.
