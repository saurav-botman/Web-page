Programme Overview

This 1-year postgraduate programme focuses on artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data-driven technologies.

Semester 1

Core Modules

  • Machine Learning (30 credits)
  • Artificial Intelligence Foundations (30 credits)
  • Data Analytics and Visualization (30 credits)

Semester 2

Core Modules

  • Deep Learning (30 credits)
  • Natural Language Processing (30 credits)
  • AI Ethics and Governance (30 credits)

Final Stage

Dissertation / Project

  • AI Research Project (60 credits)
